## Key Mechanics Learned

### New Conditions Encountered

**Rage**:
- Stacks with each application
- At start of turn with rage: must shift toward closest creature and make free strike
- Resets to 0 at end of combat or when certain thresholds reached
- Can damage allies if they're closest
- Party successfully avoided friendly fire by positioning

**Bleeding**:
- Lose 1d6 + character level in stamina when taking actions/triggered actions/tests
- Can be removed by healing effects or prayer abilities
- Different from "dying and bleeding" condition
- Only applies while actively using abilities

**Frightened** (detailed):
- Cannot willingly move closer to source
- Take banes on attacks against source (can be cancelled by buffs)
- Can be ended with save at end of turn
- Powerful crowd control effect

**Taunted**:
- Target takes double banes on attacks against creatures other than source
- Forces tactical focus on taunter
- Short duration (until end of taunter's next turn)

### Boss Mechanics

**Villain Actions**:
- Bosses can act multiple times per round
- Werewolf had 2 turns per round (standard action + villain action)
- Makes bosses extremely dangerous even when outnumbered

**Size Changes**:
- Size 2: Takes up 4 squares (2x2), larger reach and threat
- Size 3: Takes up 9 squares (3x3), huge threat range
- Larger size = more damage on area attacks

**Malice Resource**:
- Boss-specific resource for powerful abilities
- Used to activate special moves and transformations
- Limited pool forces strategic choices

**Wall Leap**:
- Signature mobility ability
- Allows repositioning without opportunity attacks normally
- Can trigger free strikes if target has appropriate abilities
- Deals damage on landing
