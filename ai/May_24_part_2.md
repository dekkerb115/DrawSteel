# D&D Session Summary - Werewolf Boss Battle

## Session Overview
After defeating the water elementals, the party pushed deeper into the cave and faced off against the werewolf in a multi-phase boss fight. The werewolf was a willing lycanthrope who had been using the cave as a lair, holding the shepherd's brother captive. The fight was intense, with the werewolf transforming mid-battle into a massive wolf form, but the party ultimately prevailed.

---

## After the Water Elementals

The party used a victory point to recover before pressing on. The next chamber was larger and unsettling - pools of water dotted the floor and the walls showed strange swirling erosion patterns that didn't look natural. Something about this place felt connected to elemental forces beyond just the river.

---

## Finding the Werewolf

Spotting a large figure in the distance standing over a body on the floor, the party identified the shepherd's brother - injured but alive. The figure was the werewolf in hybrid form, half-human and half-wolf. Before the party could act, it spoke in a raspy voice, boasting that it had once been a weak human and revelling in the power lycanthropy had given it. This was no unwilling victim of the curse - it had embraced the transformation.

Bree attempted to sneak closer for a better position but slipped on the smooth, water-worn rock and made enough noise to alert the werewolf. It immediately grabbed the brother as a hostage and combat began.

---

## The Boss Fight

### Phase 1 - Hybrid Form
The werewolf opened aggressively, biting the hostage to inflict lycanthropy before turning on the party. It used its wall leap ability to close distance quickly, launching itself off the cave walls and crashing into Bree, knocking her prone. It hit hard and fast, and at the end of the round let out a howl that frightened several party members - Bree and Vivian both found themselves unable to willingly approach it.

The party fought back effectively despite the chaos. Vivian used a powerful strained ability to force Bree into a second signature attack, dealing enough damage to leave the werewolf visibly winded. Bo marked it to help the whole party land their hits, and Aaron began spending piety to keep everyone standing through healing and condition removal.

### Phase 2 - Full Wolf Form
Mid-fight the werewolf transformed fully, growing from a large hybrid creature into a massive pure wolf that dominated the battlefield. The size increase made its area attacks hit harder and wider. Both Bo and Aaron were left bleeding and prone from a ripping claw strike.

Aaron's prayers proved critical here - he healed himself and Bree, removed bleeding and prone conditions from multiple party members, and dealt holy damage to the werewolf. Vivian turned the werewolf's own fear against it, using a psychic ability that left the werewolf frightened of her in return. When it tried to flee by leaping across the chamber, it ran through melee range of three party members who all landed free strikes on it.

### The Killing Blow
Bo delivered the finishing blow with a pommel strike to the werewolf's skull. On death, the massive wolf form shrank back down to its hybrid shape. The party had won.

---

## Aftermath

### The Shepherd's Brother
Found whimpering nearby, bitten twice during the fight. His wounds were already healing faster than they should - a clear sign he had contracted lycanthropy. The party recognized the same signs they'd seen with Mikael the carpenter. With the werewolf now dead, they had the key ingredient needed for the cure.

### Loot Acquired
- **Werewolf remains** - harvested for the lycanthropy cure (Wilhelm carried the carcass)
- **Seagrass** - found growing on the cave walls, roughly 1 pound, ingredient for an alchemical recipe
- **Wool** - received from the shepherd as promised payment, earmarked for Jeb's sweater

### Victory Rewards
- 2 total victory points earned (1 from water elementals, 1 from the werewolf)
- Party in reasonable shape with enough resources for one more encounter if needed
